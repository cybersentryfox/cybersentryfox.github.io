About Me

 

🚀 Securing the Future, One Threat at a Time.

Cybersecurity isn’t just about defending networks. It’s about staying ahead of threats, understanding vulnerabilities, and protecting what matters most. My focus lies in analyzing security reports, identifying patterns in cyber threats, and providing insights that strengthen cyber defenses.

I thrive in high-pressure environments where every second counts and enjoy the challenge of breaking down complex security incidents to uncover their root cause. While I’m early in my career, I’ve spent extensive time researching real-world breaches, refining my investigative skills, and building my cybersecurity portfolio through hands-on projects.

My reports simulate real-world incident response and risk assessment, showcasing my ability to detect threats, analyze attack patterns, and develop actionable security recommendations. While company names are fictional for educational purposes, the incidents, risks, and infrastructure challenges are drawn from actual cybersecurity events affecting critical sectors.

I also have experience studying and conducting cybersecurity gap assessments, particularly within critical infrastructure and operational technology systems. These projects involve analyzing security controls, highlighting potential risks, and recommending improvements to enhance cyber defense strategies.


> _Curiosity Fuels Security. Knowledge Builds Defense._

🛡️ Core Areas of Expertise:

  • Incident Reporting & Threat Intelligence
  • Data-Driven Root Cause Analysis
  • Cyber Threat Monitoring
  • Risk Assessment & Mitigation Strategies
  • Cybersecurity Gap Assessments
