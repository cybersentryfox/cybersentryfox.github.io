About Me

 

🚀 Securing the Future, One Threat at a Time.

Cybersecurity isn’t just about defending networks. It’s about staying ahead of threats, understanding vulnerabilities, and protecting what matters most. My focus lies in analyzing security reports, identifying patterns in cyber threats, and providing insights that strengthen cyber defenses.


I specialize in cybersecurity risk assessments and certification readiness, especially for small businesses, nonprofits, and solo entrepreneurs, with a clear focus on CyberSecure Canada, CPCC, and ISO 27001. My approach is built on clarity and practicality. I don’t overwhelm clients with jargon or enterprise frameworks. Instead, I deliver custom reports, policy drafts, and visual dashboards that make sense for real-world teams, no matter their size or tech background.


I also bring experience from analyzing cybersecurity risks in critical infrastructure sectors such as energy and utilities. This background helps me apply real-world insight when supporting CPCC-aligned efforts and organizations securing high-impact systems.


You don’t need a big consulting firm or a cybersecurity degree to get started. You just need someone who knows what matters, explains it clearly, and gives you the tools to move forward. That’s the kind of support I aim to provide.


> _Curiosity Fuels Security. Knowledge Builds Defense._

🛡️ Core Areas of Expertise:

  • Cybersecurity Gap Assessments for CPCC & CyberSecure Canada

  • Risk Analysis & Mitigation Planning

  • Audit Readiness Documentation (ISO 27001, GRC)

  • Interactive Dashboards for Security Insights (Power BI, Google Sheets, Tableau)

  • Executive Summaries & Board-Ready Policy Drafts
